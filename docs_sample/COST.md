# COST

## Financial Cost Estimate for VAS PoC

### Team

- 1 Lead Developer/Researcher (full-time): €80k–€100k (gross salary incl. taxes/social contributions)
- 1 Security/Systems Engineer (part-time): €30k–€50k

Total Personnel: €100k–€150k

### Hardware & Compute

- 2-3 Developemt Systems  + accessories: €3k–€5k
- Local GPU server (RTX 4090/2× A100 or equivalent for training small hybrids): €10k–€15k (or rent)
- EuroHPC cloud credits (pilot phase): €5,000–€10,000 (grants possible)
- Miscellaneous (storage, networking): €2,000

Total Hardware/Compute: €20k–€35k

### Software & Services

- Development tools, cloud credits (AWS/Azure/GCP for fallback), Redis hosting: €2,000–€4,000
- Domain, GitLab CI/CD, basic CI infrastructure: €1,000

Total Software/Services: €3,000 – €5,000

### Travel & Misc
- Conferences, meetings, office supplies): €3,000 – €5,000


### Grand Total PoC Estimate 
- €150k–€200k*
